About This Site

Israeli Events Atlanta is a community platform for Israelis and the broader Jewish community in Metro Atlanta, Georgia.

We created this site to solve a simple problem: events in our community are scattered across WhatsApp groups, Facebook pages, and word of mouth. There was no single place to see what's happening.

This site is open to everyone in the Israeli and Jewish community. Anyone can submit an event - no account needed. Just fill out the form and your event will appear on the list.

Have questions or feedback? Reach out via the community WhatsApp group.
